Privacy Policy

Instant Note By BetterMinds keeps your focus data local-first.

Effective date: 2026-04-03. Instant Note By BetterMinds is designed so plans, notes, completed sessions, and selected share photos stay on your device unless you choose to share content yourself.

Back to legal home View audio sources

What Instant Note By BetterMinds stores

  • Plans, notes, and completed sessions are stored on your device.
  • Selected Photo Accent images are used locally to render a share card preview and export.
  • Audio mixes and premium state are used to run the app experience you choose.

What Instant Note By BetterMinds does not upload to its own server

  • Note text.
  • Plan text.
  • Completed session notes.
  • Photo Accent image contents.

Photos and sharing

When you pick a photo, Instant Note By BetterMinds uses it on-device to compose the share card. The photo remains local until you decide to share the exported image through iOS share features.

Background audio

Instant Note By BetterMinds can continue audio playback while the app is in the background when that feature is available in the app build.

Purchases and subscriptions

Premium purchases and subscription status are handled through the App Store and RevenueCat. Instant Note By BetterMinds may keep an anonymous app user identifier on-device so purchase access can be restored on the same install or device flow.

Children, health, and sensitive claims

Instant Note By BetterMinds is a productivity and ambient audio app. It should not be presented as a medical, therapeutic, or scientifically guaranteed treatment tool unless those claims are separately substantiated.

Questions

Use the support contact listed for Instant Note By BetterMinds in App Store Connect or on the app listing if you have privacy questions.
