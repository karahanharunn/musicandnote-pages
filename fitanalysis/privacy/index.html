Privacy Policy

Fitanalysis uses a public privacy page and an anonymous in-app identity.

Effective date: 2026-04-04. Fitanalysis uses Supabase for app data, forwards body-analysis photos through the backend to Google's Gemini service for analysis, and relies on the App Store plus RevenueCat for subscriptions.

Body analysis photos

When you choose body analysis, the selected photo is uploaded to the backend so it can be processed for AI analysis. The photo is sent through the app's Supabase backend and forwarded to Google's Gemini service to generate the result.

Saved results

Fitanalysis stores body-analysis results, workout history, programs, and journey progress in Supabase so they can appear in the app later. The current body-analysis save flow stores the analysis result and metrics, not a photo URL in the app database.

Anonymous identity

Fitanalysis creates a random anonymous app identifier on your device. It is used to restore purchases and bootstrap an anonymous backend session, and it is not tied to your name or email.

Subscriptions

Subscriptions are processed by the App Store and RevenueCat. Fitanalysis does not store payment information. RevenueCat receives anonymous purchase events to manage premium entitlements.

No product analytics SDK

Fitanalysis does not use a product analytics SDK to track behavior across the app. Development builds may include debug logs for testing, and release builds keep only minimal runtime logs for lifecycle milestones and errors.

Questions

Use the support contact listed on the app's App Store page if you have privacy questions.
