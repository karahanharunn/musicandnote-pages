Privacy Policy

FitnessAppSwift uses an anonymous in-app identity and a public privacy page.

Effective date: 2026-04-09. FitnessAppSwift uses Supabase for app data, sends body-analysis photos through the backend to an AI provider for analysis, and uses the App Store plus RevenueCat for subscriptions.

Body analysis photos

When you choose body analysis, the selected photo is uploaded to the backend so it can be processed for AI analysis. The photo is sent through the app's Supabase backend and forwarded to the configured AI provider to generate the result.

Saved results

FitnessAppSwift stores body-analysis results, workout history, saved programs, and progress data so they can appear in the app later. The app stores the outputs and related metrics needed for the experience.

Anonymous identity

FitnessAppSwift creates a random anonymous app identifier on your device. It is used to restore purchases and support an anonymous backend session, and it is not tied to your name or email unless you contact support directly.

Subscriptions

Subscriptions are processed by the App Store and RevenueCat. FitnessAppSwift does not store payment information. RevenueCat receives purchase events needed to manage premium entitlements.

Health data access

If you grant optional Apple Health access, FitnessAppSwift reads only the health data needed for the features you choose to use. You can change those permissions at any time from your device settings.

No ad-tech tracking

FitnessAppSwift does not use third-party ad tracking to follow you across apps or websites. Development builds may include limited debug logging, while release builds keep only minimal runtime logs for reliability and error handling.
